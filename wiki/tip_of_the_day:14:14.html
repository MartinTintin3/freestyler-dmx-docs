Inverse channel.
A channel can be inversed. This can be handy when a pan or tilt channel are moving in the opposite direction as the other fixture.
The “Inverse” can be used on other channels than pan and tilt.
If used on other channels then it has to be done carefully because some channels may be messed up.
Channel with fixed value like gobo or color wheels are to be avoided but linear channels like RGB, CMY can be changed without worries.

Inverse can be found under Advanced Fixture Settings, Panel Description - Slider menu. and Output Menu.

More tips See Tip of the day.
