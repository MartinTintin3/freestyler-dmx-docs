Led Parcan Tutorial




In this example a Chauvet ColoradoZoom Tour is used.
It is chosen because it has a wide range of DMX channel settings and this should cower almost all types of LED parcan.

All configurations will be covered with a unique fixture. So it is possible have all as an example.
Follow the links bellow to see how the fixtures are made.

Red, Green and Blue, 3 Channel control
ARC1

ARC1+D:
Red, Green, Blue and Dimmer, 4 Channel control
ARC1 + D

ARC2:
Red, Green, Blue and White, 4 Channel control
ARC2

Red, Green, Blue, White and Dimmer, 5 Channel control

ARC2+S:

ARC2 + S

HSV:
Hue, saturation and Value. 3 Channel control
