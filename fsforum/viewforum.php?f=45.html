FreeStyler DMX

FreeStyler DMX is made solely by Rapheal Wellekens from Belgium. It's a free and versatile DMX software with an army of supporters, It has a variety of features including :

  • 2 Universe (Max 1024 channels)
  • Built in Chaser for LED and Standard Fixtures
  • 16Bit Fine control
  • MIDI Support
  • Touchscreen Support
  • Location Files (Take your laptop to many venues and jump straight in by loading up all your fixtures etc for that venue)
  • SunTriX and LedTriX Matrix features for LED Bars and large groups of fixtures
  • Advanced Patterns for Pan/Tilt Fixtures
  • Fog/Smoke Control Window
  • MEVP Compatible (FS Comes with Sunlight Magic 3D Easy View for Visualising your lighting designs in 3D)
  • Sound to Light support
  • Create Martin 2510 files and upload them

* Fixture Creator for fast and quick creation of New fixture files

  • Support Forum and Large Wiki for support

Hardware Requirements

  • PC computer working under Windows XP or above
  • 800 Mhz speed processor
  • 512 MB Memory (RAM)
  • Video card supporting Microsoft Direct 3D with 32 Mb Memory
  • 800*600 pixel screen
